## KEY THEMES WITH CREDIBILITY

### 1. [FACT] Wallace affair over priority (1858) — 90%
Alfred Russel Wallace sent Darwin an essay describing natural selection theory almost identical to Darwin's 20-year work. Darwin wrote: "I would rather burn my whole book than that he should think I behaved in a paltry spirit". Source: au-research_dark_drama.md.

### 2. [FACT] Evolution theory confirmed by DNA — 95%  
DNA confirms common origin of all life forms - every cell contains the same genetic code. Genetic analyses confirm exactly the evolutionary tree Darwin predicted based on observations. DNA comparisons show continuous accumulation of mutations across generations. Source: au-research_reality_wisdom.md.

### 3. [FACT] Gen Z connects theory with AI and social media — 85%
STEMTok has 110 billion views, youth compare social media algorithms to Darwinian selection ("only best content survives on TikTok"), AI evolution memes 2024-2025, evolution simulation games revival, gaming culture with Pokemon/Digimon evolution nostalgia. Source: au-research_youth_digital.md.

### 4. [DISPUTE] Theory misuse for racism and eugenics — 80%
Darwin never supported social Darwinism, but his theory was misused to justify imperialism, racism, eugenics and laissez-faire capitalism. Pseudoscientific theories about "racial superiority" leading to Holocaust. Contemporary science rejects any hierarchies based on origin. Source: au-research_culture_impact.md.
