## Platform-Specific Recommendations

### Facebook/Instagram (🔴 WYSOKIE RYZYKO OGÓLNE)

**UNIKAĆ**:
- Bezpośrednich cytatów o bluźnierstwie
- Dyskusji o homoerotycznych interpretacjach
- Kontrowersyjnych aspektów religijnych
- Graficznych opisów przemocy wobec zwierząt

**BEZPIECZNE PODEJŚCIE**:
- Fokus na literackie innowacje i kulturowy wpływ
- Pozytywne aspekty: przyjaźń, różnorodność załogi
- Edukacyjne treści o historii i biografii

**REKOMENDACJE**:
- Age-restrict wszystkie treści na 18+
- Używaj euphemizmów zamiast bezpośrednich opisów

- Unikaj kontrowersyjnych interpretacji współczesnych

**CONTENT WARNINGS**:
- Disclaimer na początku o mature themes
- Age-restriction na 16+ dla treści o przemocy/traumie
- Educational framing dla wszystkich kontrowersyjnych tematów

**BEZPIECZNE TEMATY**:

- Political interpretations (imperializm amerykański)

### YouTube Red Flags  
- Text-to-speech content bez human commentary
- Repetitive format używany dla multiple books
- Mass-produced content bez original value
- AI-generated scripts bez human editing

### TikTok Red Flags
- Content shorter than 1 minute (no monetization)
- Reposting other creators' book content
- Graphic content bez age restriction
- Controversial topics bez educational framing

### Universal Red Flags
- Glorification of violence lub destructive behavior
- Misinformation about historical events
- Copyright violations (long book excerpts)
- Hate speech lub discriminatory interpretations

## Crisis Management Preparedness

### If Content Gets Flagged

#### Immediate Response
1. **Document the flagging reason**
2. **Prepare educational context defense**
3. **Gather academic sources supporting content**
4. **Create appeal highlighting educational purpose**
5. **Have backup versions ready with additional disclaimers**

#### Platform-Specific Appeals

**Facebook/Instagram**: Emphasize educational value, historical context, and literary analysis purpose. Provide academic citations.

**YouTube**: Highlight human oversight, original commentary, educational framing, and compliance with authentic content policies.

**TikTok**: Stress original creation, educational purpose, appropriate age-restrictions, and community guidelines compliance.

### Content Modification Strategies

#### High-Risk Content Adaptations
- Replace direct quotes with paraphrased summaries
- Add more historical context and disclaimers
- Focus on literary technique rather than controversial themes
- Use academic language to elevate discourse level
- Include multiple scholarly perspectives

#### Alternative Presentation Methods
- Comparative analysis with other classics
- Focus on writing innovations rather than content
- Emphasize positive themes (friendship, diversity, perseverance)
- Modern relevance rather than historical controversy
- Cultural impact rather than problematic elements

### Overall Strategy
**FOCUS ON EDUCATION, NOT SENSATION**. Position all content as scholarly literary analysis rather than controversial topic exploration. The book contains mature themes that require careful handling, but with proper context and warnings, most content can be safely shared across platforms.
