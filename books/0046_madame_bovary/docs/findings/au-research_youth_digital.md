## Wnioski: Dlaczego Madame Bovary Rezonuje z Gen Z

1. **Chronic Dissatisfaction**: Emma's never-ending search dla fulfillment mirrors Gen Z's social media-driven discontent
2. **Aesthetic Obsession**: Focus na appearance over substance reflects TikTok culture
3. **Economic Anxiety**: Financial struggles from lifestyle expectations remain relevant
4. **Mental Health**: Themes of depression, anxiety, i toxic relationships highly current
5. **Identity Through Consumption**: Buying personality traits through material goods
6. **Romanticization Critique**: Warning przeciw excessive idealization relevant dla social media age

**Key Insight**: Emma Bovary nie jest outdated - she's prototype dla digital age struggles. Her novel serves jako perfect case study dla understanding contemporary youth challenges z identity, consumption, i social media-driven expectations.

**Recommendation**: Package Madame Bovary jako "The First Influencer Novel" - showing how problems of self-presentation, lifestyle curation, i reality vs. online persona existed long before social media, making classical literature surprisingly relevant dla digital natives.
